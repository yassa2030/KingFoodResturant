Frequently Asked Questions
📦 How do I track my order?
Go to My Orders page to see real-time status of all your orders.
💳 What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, and cash on delivery. Save your cards securely in your profile.
🚚 How long does delivery take?
Delivery usually takes 30-45 minutes depending on your location and order volume.
🔄 Can I cancel or modify my order?
You can cancel within 5 minutes of placing. Contact support for modifications.
🎟️ How do I use a promo code?
Enter your code in the cart page before checkout. Each code can be used once per account.
🪑 How do I book a table?
Visit the Book Table page, select date/time and number of guests.
